## Full description (4000 char max — current ~2,400)

```
TallyShot is the receipt scanner that doesn't lose your receipts.

Photograph a receipt, our AI extracts merchant, date, items, tax, and total in seconds, and your expense is tracked. No typing. No accounts. No ads. No tracking. Receipts and photos stay on your device.

Built for self-employed people, freelancers, and anyone who's tired of receipt apps that hide a paywall after the first scan.


WHAT MAKES TALLYSHOT DIFFERENT

• Never lose a receipt. If AI extraction fails, the photo is saved as "Needs Review" with a Retry button. Other apps quietly drop failed scans — we don't.

• Line item extraction. Most receipt apps capture the total but skip the items. We extract every line, store them, and export them in CSV and PDF.

• Region-aware tax. Pick your region (UK, EU, US, Australia, NZ, Canada) and we apply the right tax mode automatically. UK VAT is inclusive. US sales tax is exclusive. We never double-count tax — a bug other apps are notorious for.

• Honest free tier. 10 AI scans per month, free, forever. Manual entry is unlimited and free. No ad pop-ups after the first scan. No bait-and-switch. The exact remaining count is shown right under the Scan button.

• Truly local. Your receipts and photos live in your phone's private storage. The only thing sent over the network is the receipt photo for AI extraction — and that's not stored on our side.

• Dark mode by default. Built for actually using at night without burning your eyes.


WHAT YOU CAN DO

• Snap a receipt with the camera, or import from your gallery
• Auto-extract: merchant, date, currency, items, subtotal, tax, total, payment method, category
• Edit any field, add or remove line items, add notes
• Mark expenses as tax-deductible (with smart defaults per category)
• See monthly spending broken down by category and merchant
• Filter by category, deductible status, or "needs review"
• Search by merchant or notes
• Export any date range to CSV (with line items) or PDF (three templates: tax submission, reimbursement, detailed)
• See deductible totals at a glance, ready for self-assessment


PRIVACY

• No account required.
• No analytics SDKs, no trackers, no ads.
• Receipt photos are sent to our AI proxy for extraction only — never logged, never used for training.
• Tap "Delete all data" in Settings to wipe everything.
• Read the full policy in-app.


PRICING

10 AI scans per month and unlimited manual entry, free, forever. A Pro tier with unlimited AI scans is coming — clearly priced, no surprises.


PERMISSIONS

• Camera — to photograph receipts.
• Internet — to send receipt photos to our AI proxy.

That's it. No location, no microphone, no contacts.


GIVE FEEDBACK

We read every email. If something is broken or missing, tell us at hello@tallyshot.app and we'll respond.
```
