Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Short version: Your receipt photos and data stay on your device. We send receipt images to an AI service to extract text — nothing else is collected, sold, or shared.

1. Who we are

TallyShot is an Android app that lets you photograph receipts and automatically extract the details using AI. It is developed and maintained as an independent application.

2. What data we collect

Data stored on your device only:

  • Receipt photos you take or import
  • Extracted receipt data (merchant, date, amount, category)
  • Your app settings (currency preference, tax mode, scan count)

Data sent off your device:

  • Receipt images are sent to our AI extraction service (a Cloudflare Worker calling the Claude API by Anthropic) solely to extract text from the image. Images are not stored by the service after processing.

Data we never collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Location data
  • Any data from your photo library beyond the specific receipt image you choose
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Analytics or crash telemetry

3. Camera and photo library

TallyShot requests access to your camera so you can photograph receipts directly in the app. It requests access to your photo library only when you explicitly tap "Choose from Gallery" — it does not browse or index your library.

You can revoke these permissions at any time in your device Settings.

4. AI extraction

When you scan a receipt, the image is sent over HTTPS to a secure processing endpoint. The service extracts text fields (merchant, date, total, line items) and returns them to the app. The image is not retained after the response is sent.

This service is powered by Anthropic's Claude API. Anthropic's own privacy practices apply to that processing — see anthropic.com/privacy.

5. Data storage

All receipt data is stored locally on your device using SQLite. We do not operate servers that store your receipts. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is deleted.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties for marketing, advertising, or analytics purposes.

The only external service your data touches is the AI extraction endpoint described in section 4 above.

7. Children

TallyShot is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Your rights

Because your data is stored only on your device, you have full control over it. You can delete individual receipts inside the app, or delete all data by uninstalling the app.

9. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
